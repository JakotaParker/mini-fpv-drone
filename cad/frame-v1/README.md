## Design Intent

Frame V1 is the first physical prototype for a mini 3-inch FPV quadcopter. It is intended to validate:

- Overall geometry
- Printability
- Arm stiffness
- Motor mounting
- Electronics mounting
- Camera placement
- Battery placement
- Propeller clearance
- Wiring access

## Known Risks

- PLA+ may be adequate for fit testing but may not be the final flight material.
- Mounting dimensions have not yet been physically validated.
- Arm stiffness and crash durability are unknown.
- Long print duration requires active supervision and printer-safety precautions.
